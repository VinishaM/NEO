Alumni Charter Days Reception

April 2015

To commemorate Cornell's 150th, the Cornell Club of Northeast Ohio presented two very special gifts to University Archivist Elaine Engst. These included 85 postcards that were sent to members of the Cleveland alumni association from 1908 to 1928 and a 100-year-old scrapbook compiled by a Cornell civil engineer and WWI veteran who spent his later life in Cleveland. These items will be added to the Kroch Library's Rare Manuscripts collection.
